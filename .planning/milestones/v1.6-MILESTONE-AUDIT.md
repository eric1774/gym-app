### Integration Note

**BLE-04 blocked-permission recovery (low severity):** `openAppSettings()` in `BLEPermissions.ts` is exported but never called. When BLE permissions are permanently denied, `HeartRateContext.startScan` silently returns. The core requirement (permissions declared and requested) is satisfied; the missing piece is the user-facing recovery UX for the `blocked` state. Classified as tech debt, not a blocker.
